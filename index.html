MakerSpace CFAL

Who We Are

CFAL Makerspace is an extracurricular club at CFAL where students can engage in hands-on projects and creative activities. With a membership of 60 students, our makerspace offers opportunities to work on projects in areas such as electronics, robotics, woodworking, and 3D printing. We provide a supportive environment for students to explore their interests, develop practical skills, and bring their ideas to life, whether for personal projects, academic pursuits, or extracurricular interests.

MakerSpace

Unlock your Inner Maker, Innovator, And Enterpereneur.

Here at MakerSpace, we are all about fostering creativity, innovation, and collaboration. Our space is designed to empower individuals to explore new ideas, learn hands-on skills, and bring their projects to life. Whether you're an artist, engineer, student, or hobbyist, MakerSpace provides the tools, resources, and community support to turn your visions into reality. From 3D printing and laser cutting to electronics and woodworking, we encourage experimentation and skill-building in a supportive and dynamic environment. Join us to unleash your creativity and make something amazing!
